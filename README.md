The concept of this course (701:Satellite Systems Design and Simulation Laboratory) was to design a complete mission 
for a fire detection satellite at a VLEO orbit in a team of 10 people, each designing a subsystem and collaborating with
the others, i took part designing the On-Board Comnputer and EPS Subsystems in the "HIVE" Mission. 
The HIVE mission is a Very Low Earth Orbit (VLEO) Earth observation constellation designed to
support early wildfire detection over Greece, while also providing extended monitoring capabilities
over Europe. Here both the final team project and my own personal are provided.
